Have you ever shopped on a website which supports a different currency and when you go to the shopping cart, the price doesn't look too bad but however, when you get your bill it surprises you because you never expected the currency exchange to be so large? I know exactly how you feel, and that is a part of the reason why I made this google chrome extension. Now with a click of a button, you can easily convert your currency before checking out your items to have a better idea to how much your items really cost.


This extension has currency rates that are updated daily. To see the source code of this project, just click here.
